Opinion

Investing in Rare-Earth Infrastructure Is a Strategic Imperative

By Marisa Chen
June 15, 2025 2 min read

The recent rare-earth export drama is not just a trade flashpoint—it's a wake-up call on industrial sovereignty.

The recent rare-earth export drama is not just a trade flashpoint—it's a wake-up call on industrial sovereignty. Continuing to rely on Chinese processing leaves U.S. industries and defense technologies at strategic risk. We must dramatically increase funding for U.S. mining, processing (like MP Materials), and international partnerships such as Greenland and Saudi ventures. True resilience demands a whole-of-government investment—because in this age, raw materials are indeed national security.
