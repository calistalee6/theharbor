Meet Lex "Lexi" Navarro

Founder & Chief Culture Officer

Lexi spent five years at BuzzFeed mastering the art of the viral listicle before realizing that pop culture deserved more thoughtful coverage. After her article "27 Things Only Late-2010s Kids Will Remember" broke the internet with 47 million views, she knew it was time to build something bigger.

"I wanted to create the publication I wished existed when I was 22 and trying to keep up with culture while working three jobs. TrendSnack is for anyone who wants to be culturally fluent without spending four hours a day on TikTok."
